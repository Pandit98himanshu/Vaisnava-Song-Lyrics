(1)
kṛṣṇa prema mayī rādhā
rādhā prema mayo hariḥ
jīvanena dhane nityaḿ
rādhā kṛṣṇa gatir mama
 
(2)
kṛṣṇasya draviṇaḿ radha
rādhāyāḥ draviṇaṇ hariḥ
jīvanena dhane nityaḿ
rādhā kṛṣṇa gatir mama

rādhā kṛṣṇa gatir mama
 
(5)

jīvanena dhane nityaḿ
rādhā kṛṣṇa gatir mama
 
(6)

(7)

(8)
vṛndāvaneśvarī rādhā

rādhā kṛṣṇa gatir mama
