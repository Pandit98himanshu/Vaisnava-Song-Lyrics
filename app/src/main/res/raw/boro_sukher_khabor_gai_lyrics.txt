(2)

śraddhā-mūlye śuddha-nāma sei hāṭete bikāy
 
(3)

(4)

(5)

(6)

śraddhā-mātra lo’ye den parama-ānanda
 
(7)

(8)

(9)

gṛhe thāko, bane thāko, nā thāke jañjāl
 
(10)
